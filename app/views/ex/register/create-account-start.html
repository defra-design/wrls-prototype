Create an account to manage your water abstraction licence online

You must be either a licence holder to create an account. Once registered licence holders can:

  • view your abstraction licence information
  • submit abstraction returns
  • give access to a named contact to submit returns on their behalf

To protect your data we will post a security code to the licence holder at their registered address.
